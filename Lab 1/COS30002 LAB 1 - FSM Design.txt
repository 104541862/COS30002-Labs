States: 
- Drilling (Costs NRG, Gain ORE)
- Selling (Costs ORE, Gain MON)
- Recharging (Costs MON, Gain NRG)

Variables: 
- Energy (NRG)
- Ore (ORE)
- Money (MON)

Drill starts with enough money to recharge and full energy so that it doesn't die before it can sell enough to recharge and become self-sustaining.
When low on energy, recharge - this takes the highest priority. With enough energy, drill ore. When ore reaches capacity of 100, sell.
